# Apex Dispatch v3 Operations Plan

## Objective

Maximize net earnings per active hour and operational mile while minimizing unpaid recovery time.

## Offer procedure

1. Enter payout, pickup, and drop-off.
2. Set the current position.
3. Run Apex analysis.
4. Listen to the recommendation and alerts.
5. Confirm the route is current.
6. The driver makes the final accept or decline decision.
7. Start spoken guidance only when safely parked.
8. After completion, follow the exact recovery point and parking instruction.

## Recovery standard

A recovery instruction is not complete unless it contains:

- Exact point name
- Full address or coordinates
- Exact marked parking area
- Recovery ETA
- Recovery miles and minutes

General descriptions such as “downtown,” “River Road,” or “the mall area” are not valid recovery instructions.

## Voice standard

- Voice alerts are enabled by default.
- English and Nicaraguan Spanish are available.
- Spoken guidance requires the app to remain open with location permission active.
- The driver may stop voice guidance at any time.

## Safety standard

Never save or use a fire lane, loading zone, restricted private stall, roadway shoulder, or obstructed area as a recovery point. Verify posted restrictions before waiting.
