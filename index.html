Route intelligence capabilities

  1. Traffic-aware mileage and duration: current location → pickup → customer → recovery.
  2. ETA: pickup, delivery, and return-to-zone arrival times.
  3. Alternatives: default route plus available alternatives for each route leg.
  4. Route preferences: avoid tolls, highways, and ferries.
  5. Recovery matrix: compare up to 10 destinations by traffic-aware time and mileage.
  6. Google Maps handoff: pickup, customer, staging, and complete route navigation links.
  7. Protected backend: Cloudflare Worker, origin allowlist, validation, short cache, and secret key storage.
